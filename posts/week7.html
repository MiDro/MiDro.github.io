Thursday July 6, 2017

We met with Prof. Matthew Spencer, an electrical engineering professor at Harvey Mudd College, to get more insight and advice on how to refine the PCB layout. We all experienced the steep learning curve when dealing with actual implementation versus textbook knowledge. Our next meeting included the SLIA employees that clean the town; they are our main audience for this project. We gathered suggestions and derived contraints that we have to keep in mind during development of the system. For example, they would prefer to receive text messages rather than use a website and they also mentioned reducing the amount of necessary data in order to access the dashboard.
